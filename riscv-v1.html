Premières Rencontres RISC-V – First RISC-V Meetings

The first RISC-V Meetings on the impact and opportunities of RISC-V were dedicated to Accademic RISC-V opportunities.

They took place on Wednesday October 17th, on CEA premises at Minatec Grenoble, France.

The program of this first edition is available online. A video summary was presented at HiPEAC CSW 2018.
